Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

The Short Version

Your prompts stay in YOUR Google Drive. We don't have access to them. We don't want access to them. We don't collect, store, or sell your data. Period.
CloudPrompt is privacy-first by design. Your data never touches our servers because we don't have any servers storing user data.

1. What Data We Collect

1.1 Data We DO NOT Collect

  • Your prompts or any content you save
  • Your personal information
  • Your browsing history
  • Analytics or tracking data
  • Any data from your Google Drive

1.2 Data Stored Locally (On Your Device Only)

  • Local Cache: A copy of your prompts for fast loading (stored in Chrome's local storage)
  • Preferences: Your settings like theme preference and default category
  • OAuth Token: Encrypted authentication token for Google Drive access
All your data stays on your device and in your Google Drive. We literally cannot see it.

2. Google Drive Integration

2.1 What We Access

CloudPrompt uses the drive.file scope, which means:

  • We can ONLY access files that CloudPrompt creates
  • We CANNOT see your other Google Drive files
  • We CANNOT access your Google Docs, Sheets, or other files

2.2 How Your Data is Stored

  • Your prompts are saved as a JSON file in your Google Drive
  • Location: CloudPrompt/prompts.json in your Drive
  • You can view, edit, or delete this file directly in Google Drive
  • The file is protected by Google's security

2.3 Revoking Access

You can disconnect CloudPrompt from your Google account at any time:

  • Go to Settings in CloudPrompt ‚Üí Click "Disconnect"
  • Or visit Google Account Permissions

3. Data Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS)
  • Google Drive data is encrypted at rest by Google
  • OAuth tokens are securely managed by Chrome
  • No data is sent to any third-party servers

4. Third-Party Services

Service Purpose Data Shared
Google OAuth Authentication None (handled by Chrome)
Google Drive API Store your prompts Your prompt data (to YOUR Drive)
Chrome Web Store Extension distribution None
We only use Google services for authentication and storage. No analytics, no tracking, no ads.

5. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data in Google Drive anytime
  • Export: Download your prompts as JSON or CSV from Settings
  • Delete: Remove individual prompts or delete the entire CloudPrompt folder
  • Disconnect: Revoke access and delete local data anytime

6. Children's Privacy

CloudPrompt is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has used CloudPrompt, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do:

  • We'll update the "Last Updated" date
  • For significant changes, we'll notify you via the extension
  • Your continued use means you accept the updated policy

8. Legal Compliance

CloudPrompt complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • Google API Services User Data Policy

Your Trust Matters

CloudPrompt was built with privacy as the #1 priority. We don't want your data, we don't need your data, and we don't collect your data. Your prompts stay in YOUR Google Drive, under YOUR control, forever.
